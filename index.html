About Me

A Multidisciplinary Professional

He is an Architect, a Computational designer and a programmer who is interested in creating technologically advanced and environmentally responsible design, He pursued a degree from the National Institute of Technology, Patna (Institute of National Importance in India).

He is passionate about design, and research, in typical workday. He enjoys the critical analysis of architectural challenges and bringing innovation as well as efficiency to the process of design. His experience of working on both geometrical as well as functional aspects of architectural design on various scales, combined with his skills of using computational tools for building analytics and simulation, enables his unique perspective on designing High-Performance Architecture driven by not just vague insights but driven by data and use of integrating advance technology (aka the use of AI/ML) within the AEC industry.

Coming from a solid technical background, his interest in both technology and construction gives him an edge over others on optimising techniques and valuation, he is a FullStack Developer and loves providing solutions that helps solve crutial challanges in the relm of Web Development.
